FLORENCY

ABOUT
project

It is a simple landing page design for selling florist services. The presentation contains the colors used for the design and several pages with cards of services, prices and special offers.

fonts and colors

This choice of colors can be explained by the fact that shades of purple and pink evokes a sense of elegance and top class. This is a way to attract wealthy customers to a one-page selling website. Light colors, especially lavender, will remind you of spring and romance. Dark tones add a certain mystery and even symbolize creativity. MARCELLUS - for headers. Manjari - for text blocks.
