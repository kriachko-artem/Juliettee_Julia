MIO CARO

ABOUT
project

It is an instructional design project for a fictional online women's clothing store of the brand called MIO CARO. The challenge was to combine a simple and user-friendly interface with a modern design. As a result, the main screen was created in three versions and a catalog with a full range of clothes, a product card, a basket, an order page and others. In work used photographs from this website www.mesdemoisellesparis.com and some from the photographer by Olga Dieterle.

fonts and colors

This choice of colors can be explained by the fact that blue is often used to represent calmness and responsibility. Light shades of blue look fresh and friendly. Dark blue looks strong and reliable. Intense blue is refreshing and energizing, navy blue is great for corporate websites or other occasions where strength and reliability are needed in designs. MAZIUS REVIEW 20.09 - for headers.
